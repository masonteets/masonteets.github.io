Here are the ingredients for Dr. Pepper

1. Carbonated water: Provides the fizziness of the drink. 2. High fructose corn syrup (or sugar): Used as a sweetener. 3. Caramel color: Adds the brown color to the beverage. 4. Phosphoric acid: Provides tartness and acts as a preservative. 5. Natural and artificial flavors: These give Dr Pepper its distinct taste. 6. Sodium benzoate: Another preservative used to maintain freshness. 7. Caffeine: Dr Pepper contains a small amount of caffeine, although the exact quantity can vary.
